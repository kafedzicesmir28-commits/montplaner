================================================================================
  QUICK SETUP GUIDE - COPY THIS SQL TO SUPABASE
================================================================================

STEP 1: Go to https://supabase.com and open your project

STEP 2: Click "SQL Editor" in the left sidebar

STEP 3: Click "New query" button

STEP 4: Open "CANONICAL_SETUP.md" (in this same folder) and follow it

STEP 5: Paste into the SQL Editor

STEP 6: Click "Run" button (or press Ctrl+Enter / Cmd+Enter)

STEP 7: You should see "Success. No rows returned" message

STEP 8: Verify tables exist:
   - Click "Table Editor" in left sidebar
   - You should see at least: employees, stores, shifts, shift_assignments, vacations, companies, profiles, login_logs

STEP 9: Create an admin user:
   - Click "Authentication" → "Users"
   - Click "Add user" → "Create new user"
   - Enter email and password
   - CHECK "Auto Confirm User"
   - Click "Create user"

STEP 10: Restart your Next.js dev server and try logging in!
